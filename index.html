Guangfu Wang

Professor, doctoral supervisor, and Vice Dean of the School of Mathematics and Information Science, Yantai University.

Basic Information

Guangfu Wang is a professor, doctoral supervisor, Huang-Hai Scholar, and Vice Dean of the School of Mathematics and Information Science at Yantai University.

He is a council member of the Graph Theory and Combinatorics Branch of the Operations Research Society of China, a council member of the China Society of Commercial Statistics, and a reviewer for Mathematical Reviews.

Research Interests

His main research interests are graph theory and its applications, including median graphs, proportions of graphs in hypercube graphs, isometric embedding problems, and distance-related graph indices.

Projects, Publications, and Awards

He has led three National Natural Science Foundation of China projects, one Shandong Provincial Natural Science Foundation project, two Jiangxi Provincial Natural Science Foundation projects, and several provincial teaching reform and research projects. He has published more than forty papers in journals including Computational Geometry, European Journal of Combinatorics, Applied Mathematics and Computation, and Discrete Mathematics.

He has authored one monograph, served as chief editor or deputy chief editor of three textbooks, and received four software copyrights. His students have won prizes in national mathematical modeling and entrepreneurship competitions. He has supervised eleven master's students, and one thesis received the 2021 Jiangxi Excellent Master's Thesis Award.
