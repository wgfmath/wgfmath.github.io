Distance-Unbalancedness of Unicyclic Graphs with Prescribed Cycle Length

Guangfu Wang, Ruhua Liu

Download PDF

Abstract

For a connected graph, distance-unbalancedness measures the total imbalance between ordered vertex pairs determined by distance comparisons. This paper studies the invariant on unicyclic graphs with prescribed cycle length. It gives a counterexample to a previously asserted lower formula, records exact endpoint cases, proves an asymptotically sharp maximum formula for fixed cycle length, and develops exhaustive rooted-tree enumeration and test families for the minimum problem.

Keywords

distance-unbalancedness; unicyclic graph; fixed cycle length; extremal graph; rooted tree enumeration.
