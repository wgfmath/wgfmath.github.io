Preprint

Metric criteria for quasi-distance-balanced direct products and nicely distance-balanced vertex-transitive graphs

Guangfu Wang, Mengmeng Chen

Download PDF

Abstract

We address two problems raised in the literature. For quasi-lambda-distance-balanced direct products, we prove a parity-based distance formula and obtain a factor-wise characterization, with explicit consequences for complete bipartite factors. For nicely distance-balanced graphs, we resolve the vertex-transitive case by showing that the property is equivalent to edge-independence of the equidistant-vertex number, yielding edge-orbit, local, Cayley graph, and Cartesian-product criteria.

Keywords

distance-balanced graph; quasi-lambda-distance-balanced graph; nicely distance-balanced graph; direct product; vertex-transitive graph; Cayley graph.
