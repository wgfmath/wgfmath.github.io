l1-embeddability of Regular Quadrilateral Maps on the Torus and the Klein bottle

Guangfu Wang, Wenchao Cong, et al.

Download PDF

Abstract

A connected graph is l1-embeddable if its graph metric admits an isometric embedding into an l1-space. This paper studies five families of regular quadrilateral maps on the torus and the Klein bottle and completely characterizes the l1-embeddable graphs in these families, identifying all exceptional embeddable cases.
