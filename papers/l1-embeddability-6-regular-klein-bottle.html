l1-Embeddability of 6-Regular Klein-Bottle Graphs

Guangfu Wang, Wenchao Cong

Download PDF

Abstract

We determine which finite simple 6-regular graphs on the Klein bottle have graph metrics that embed isometrically into l1. Using Negami's classification and its comparison with Thomassen's notation, all such graphs are organized into three standard families, denoted Kh(p, q), Kce(p, q) and Kco(p, q). We prove that the unique isometrically l1-embeddable graph among them is Kco(5, 4). The non-exceptional cases are excluded by explicit five-point hypermetric certificates, while the positive case is realized by an explicit cut decomposition with eight cuts. As a quantitative refinement, the same certificates give explicit lower bounds for the least l1-distortion c1(G) of every non-exceptional graph.

Keywords

6-regular graph; Klein bottle; graph metric; l1-embedding; cut metric; hypermetric inequality; distortion.
