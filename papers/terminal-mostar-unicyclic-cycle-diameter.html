Abstract

The Mostar index is a distance-based topological index measuring the distance imbalance of the two end-vertices of an edge. Motivated by the terminal Mostar index of trees, this paper extends the definition to connected graphs by counting pendent vertices only. The invariant is studied on unicyclic graphs, with extremal values and structures determined under fixed order, prescribed cycle length, prescribed diameter, and simultaneous restrictions.
