Inverse Edge Mostar Indices of c-Cyclic Molecular Graphs

Guangfu Wang, Yimei Gao

Download PDF

Abstract

The edge Mostar index is a distance-based bond-additive invariant measuring imbalance between numbers of bonds closer to the two end-vertices of an edge. This paper answers an inverse problem for fixed cyclomatic number by constructing c-cyclic molecular graphs whose edge Mostar indices realize all sufficiently large positive integers, with explicit families and edge-distance classifications.

Keywords

edge Mostar index; molecular graph; inverse problem; cyclomatic number; chemical graph theory.
