A Proof of the Tratnik-Ye Medianity Conjecture for Resonance Graphs of Perfect Matchings on Surfaces

Guangfu Wang, Ke Kang

Download PDF

Abstract

Tratnik and Ye conjectured that if a graph is embedded in a closed surface and a proper set of even faces is chosen, then every connected component of the corresponding resonance graph is a median graph. This paper proves the conjecture by constructing a cubical complex for each connected component and showing that it is a CAT(0) cube complex whose 1-skeleton is the resonance graph component.

Keywords

perfect matching; resonance graph; median graph; CAT(0) cube complex; graph on a surface.
